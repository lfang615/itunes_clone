No Lines = No Waiting

Our flights take off from private airports so you can avoid check-in lines and skip the TSA. We recommend that our passangers arrive just 15 minutes before their flight time.

Personal Concierge

Nothing is more important to our team than our client's satisfaction. Whether you want flowers for a special someone or need a luxury car when you land, we are committed to prividing world-class amenities to our clients.

First Class Experience

We hand pick the private jets you fly in from our operators, so from the moment you step on board to the moment you land you will experience complete luxury.

Community

We like to promote networking whether you are on the ground or in the air because you will always be surrounded by wide variety of individuals from their respective fields.

Shareable Memberships

All of you membership options are structured to allow multiple people to share a single membership.

No Hidden Fees

No fees for parking, baggaging, last-minute reservations, cancellations, snacks, etc. You pay for membership, everything else is included.
